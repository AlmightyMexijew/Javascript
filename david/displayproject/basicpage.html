Gershon Tepper web services can help! We offer web design, development, social media management, and basic security posture review. Contact us NOW to set up a meeting!

    Our services that we provide:
  • Web Design: The most important step in any web based activity. Without a design your page is at risk of coming out patchy, disorganized, and full of bugs. It becomes harder to maintain, leading to higher maintenance costs overall. A strong simple design can mean a more effective sale, a friendlier user experience, and cheaper cost when the time comes to update.

  • Web Development: The coding step. All work is done by hand to customize and to minimize the flaws and bugs of automatic coding. In the event that you need a CMS(Content Management System) like Wordpress we are happy to set that up as well. The most important thing is that everything is done right and cleanly. Only code you actually need for a faster, safer, cleaner website.

  • Social Media Management: If you are a businessperson you don't have time to spend important hours of the work day organizing social media campaigns across many resources and answering the barrage of questions. We simplify this step by organizing into general advertisement, special coordinated events, and establishing policies to answer the most common and relevant questions for you.

  • Basic Security Posture Review: Security is important. As web technology gets more and more complicated the average website is no longer simply HTML. Your website may include technologies such as Wordpress, JavaScript, PHP, SQL databases, and more. These Frankenstein-like arrangements can lead to security flaws that impact both you and your customers. You may even be liable for failing to have what are considered the minimum standard features of web security such as encrypted traffic and least access policy. Our Basic Security Posture Review is NOT a penetration test but a lighter review of common issues present in a website that may warrant further exploration. This option puts the power in your hands to understand what avenues of attack exist and whether there is reasonable concern warranting a more detailed professional penetration test assessment.
